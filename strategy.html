ELITE TRADING STRATEGY

Proven BTC/USDT swing trading with systematic risk management

CORE PLAY

0.2 BTC Per Position

Consistent position sizing across all trades. Both Long and Short directions for market-neutral approach. 99% focus on BTC/USDT for laser-sharp execution.

Smart DCA (1-2 Steps)

Dollar-Cost Average into positions on market dips. Add 1-2 times to lower average entry cost. Maximum 2 DCA steps per position to control risk.

x10 Leverage

Amplify gains with controlled leverage. x10 provides optimal risk/reward for BTC swing trading. Always use stop-losses to protect downside.

5%+ Profit Targets

Systematic profit taking starting at 5%. Scale targets based on market conditions - sometimes hold for 10-15% on strong trends. Never get greedy.

Swing Trading Style

Hold positions for days to weeks to ride major trends. Occasionally momentum plays for quick entries/exits on strong signals. Patience is key.

RISK MANAGEMENT

Stop-Loss Protection

Always use stop-losses at 3-4% below entry (for longs) or above entry (for shorts). Never risk more than 2% per trade.

Position Correlation

Maximum 5 concurrent positions. Avoid overexposure in same direction. Balance longs and shorts based on market analysis.

TRADING SLANG 101

GO LONG
Buy position betting price goes up. Profit when BTC price increases.
SHORT IT
Sell position betting price goes down. Profit when BTC price decreases.
DCA
Dollar-Cost Averaging - Add more to position at lower prices to reduce average entry cost.
LEVERAGE
Using borrowed funds to amplify position size. x10 means 10x exposure with 1x capital.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why 99% BTC/USDT focus?
Bitcoin has the highest liquidity, clearest trends, and most predictable patterns. Specializing in one pair allows for deeper analysis and better execution. No distraction from altcoins' noise.
What about market crashes?
Crashes are actually opportunities. The strategy includes short positions to profit from downturns. DCA allows averaging into BTC at discount prices during panic sells.
How do you handle sideways markets?
Reduce position size and frequency during choppy/sideways periods. Sometimes it's better to sit in cash and wait for clear trends. Patience beats overtrading.
Can I customize the strategy?
Yes! You can adjust position size, leverage, and profit targets when setting up copy trading. But understand the risk-reward implications of any changes.
What's the minimum to start?
Technically you can start with 0.2 BTC + margin for x10 leverage (~2 BTC total). But recommend having at least 5 BTC for proper risk management across multiple positions.
